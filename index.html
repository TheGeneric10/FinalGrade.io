Weighted Grade Calculation Explanation

Example (Weights in Percent):

Math: 80, Weight: 30%
Biology: 90, Weight: 50%
History: 72, Weight: 20%

Weighted Grade = (0.30 × 80) + (0.50 × 90) + (0.20 × 72) = 24 + 45 + 14.4 = 83.4

Example (Weights in Points/Hours):

Math: 80, Weight: 3 points
Biology: 90, Weight: 5 points
History: 72, Weight: 2 points

Weighted Grade = (3×80 + 5×90 + 2×72) / (3+5+2) = (240 + 450 + 144) / 10 = 834 / 10 = 83.4
